Shrimp Stir Fry

Description

My take on stir fry. This is something I make from memory. It usually varies every time I make it.

I usually center it around a gingery, citrus, garlic flavor. Hopefully you enjoy it!

Ingredients

  • Pack of Asian Noodle(Personal Preference- I prefer Udon)
    • Chow Mein
    • Egg Noodles
    • Udon
    • Rice Noodles
  • 1 Package of Shrimp
  • 1 Broccoli Floret
  • 1 can Water Chestnuts
  • 1 Can Baby Corn
  • Small bunch Bean Sprouts
  • 2 Celery Sticks, Chopped
  • 1 Onion, Chopped
  • 2 Bell Peppers, chopped
  • 1 Large Carrot, chopped
  • 8 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 small piece of Ginger, minced
  • 1 Cup Soy Sauce
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 1 Lime, squeezed
  • 1 tsp Brown Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Red Pepper Flakes

Making the Stir Fry

  1. In a large frying pan over medium/high heat, put 2 Tbsp of Olive Oil, minced Garlic and Ginger, Onion, Shrimp until Shrimp is cooked.
  2. Once Shrimp is cooked, add in remaining vegetables.
    • Carrots
    • Water Chestnuts
    • Broccoli
    • Bean Sprouts
    • Bell Peppers
    • Celery
    • Baby Corn
    Cook vegetables for a couple minutes.
  3. Add in Soy Sauce, Pepper Flakes, Brown Sugar, Lime, lower heat and bring to a simmer, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add Salt & Pepper to taste.
  5. While pan simmers, bring a separate pot with water to a boil.
  6. Once Boiling, add preferred noodle and let cook until slightly undercooked(Will Finish in the pan).
  7. Once noodles are finished, drain water and add noodle in to the pan.
  8. Mix noddles, vegetables and shrimp well, and let cook for additional 3 minutes, letting the noodles soak in the sauce.
    • You Can also serve noodles and stir fry separately. Just scoop Stir Fry on top!
  9. Serve and Enjoy!!
